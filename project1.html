I am Aditya, a passionate engineer with a degree in Computer Science from the University of Mumbai. I am keen to learn new technologies and have developed an interest in the fields of Web Development and Data Analytics. During my initial years of engineering, I had done an internship in Web Development from Suven Consultants and Technology Pvt. Ltd. During the internship, I created a website of a Shopping Cart in which the user can view the products available online and can add to the cart if he/she wishes to buy them and even remove it from the cart after looking the bill. On the other side, the admin can add new products, by filling a simple form. In this project, I learned the use of various front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, PHP, JavaScript and back-end technologies such as PHP, XML and AJAX and had my first practical experience in creating a full-fledged project. Also, My-SQL database was used to store the products in the database. I also joined Face Prep's Front End Web Development Bootcamp in which I learnt the basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript and coded projects such as a Number-Guessing game, Live-Box Generator and determining the position of the queen in Chess game.
